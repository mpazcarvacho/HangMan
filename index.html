💣 How to Play 💣

1️⃣ Click the Play button 👆 2️⃣ A random word will be selected and hidden from you. You have to guess the word by guessing a letter at a time. 3️⃣ Each letter you guess correctly will be shown on all the places it can be found in your incognita word. 4️⃣You have 6 attempts to guess the word before your man gets burned! (and therefore you lose the game).
